Jobs at the Crossroads

AI is changing the work inside jobs before it replaces job titles.

Across industries, AI is already drafting text, classifying requests, summarizing records, screening patterns, and supporting decisions. Some tasks are becoming faster or more automated, while others still depend on judgment, care, context, and accountability.

This story follows how job postings reflect that shift: where AI adoption is growing, which roles show higher AI intensity, how salaries move, where automation risk concentrates, and where reskilling demand appears.
